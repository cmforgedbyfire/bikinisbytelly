Returns & Exchanges

Our Policy

Custom-Made Items

Because each bikini is handmade to order with your specific measurements and color choices, we cannot accept returns or exchanges for change of mind.

Quality Issues

If there's a quality issue or defect with your bikini, please contact us within 7 days of receiving your order.

We'll work with you to make it right - whether that's a replacement, repair, or refund.

Fit Issues

If your bikini doesn't fit as expected and you followed our size guide:

  • Contact us within 7 days of receipt
  • Provide photos showing the fit issue
  • We'll work with you on alterations or a remake

Wrong Item Received

If you received the wrong item, contact us immediately. We'll send you the correct item at no additional cost.

Damaged in Shipping

If your bikini arrives damaged:

  • Take photos of the damage and packaging
  • Contact us within 48 hours
  • We'll file a claim and send a replacement

Need to Start a Return or Exchange?

Contact us at bikinisbytelly@outlook.com

Include your order number and photos if applicable.
