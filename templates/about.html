About Bikinis By Telly

Handcrafted swimwear made with love

Our Story

Welcome to Bikinis By Telly! Each piece in our collection is lovingly handcrafted, combining traditional sewing and crochet techniques to create unique, beautiful bikinis that you won't find anywhere else.

Every bikini is made to order, ensuring you get the perfect fit and your chosen colors. We take pride in our craftsmanship and attention to detail, using only premium materials that are comfortable, durable, and look amazing.

Whether you choose one of our signature designs or work with us to create something completely custom, you're getting a one-of-a-kind piece made just for you.

What Makes Us Different

Made to your measurements for a comfortable, flattering fit

Premium Materials

High-quality fabrics and yarns that look great and last

The Creation Process

When you order a bikini from us, here's what happens:

  1. Order Placed: You choose a design or submit a custom request
  2. Materials Selected: We source the perfect colors and materials for your piece
  3. Handcrafted: Your bikini is carefully sewn or crocheted (2-3 weeks)
  4. Quality Check: Every detail is inspected to ensure perfection
  5. Shipped: Your unique bikini is carefully packaged and sent to you
