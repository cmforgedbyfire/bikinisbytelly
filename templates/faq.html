Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to make a bikini?

Each bikini is handmade to order and typically takes 2-3 weeks to complete. During busy seasons, this may extend to 3-4 weeks. We'll provide an estimated completion date when you place your order.

Can I request custom colors?

Absolutely! While we love blue tones, we can work with any color you'd like. Just let us know your preferences when ordering, and we'll source the perfect materials.

What materials do you use?

We use premium swimwear fabrics that are chlorine and salt-water resistant, as well as high-quality yarns for our crocheted pieces. All materials are chosen for durability, comfort, and vibrant color.

Do you offer custom sizing?

Yes! Since each bikini is made to order, we can customize the fit to your exact measurements at no extra charge. Just provide your measurements when ordering.

What's your return/exchange policy?

Because each piece is custom-made, we don't accept returns for change of mind. However, if there's an issue with the fit or quality, please contact us within 7 days of receiving your order and we'll work with you to make it right.

How do I care for my handmade bikini?

Hand wash in cold water and lay flat to dry. Avoid wringing or twisting. Rinse after use in chlorine or salt water. With proper care, your bikini will last for many seasons!

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we ship within the United States. International shipping may be available - please contact us to discuss.

What if I need my order by a specific date?

Contact us before ordering! We'll do our best to accommodate rush orders when possible. There may be an additional fee for expedited production.

Can I see examples of your work?

Yes! Check out our product gallery and follow us on Instagram @bikinisbytelly for the latest creations and customer photos.
