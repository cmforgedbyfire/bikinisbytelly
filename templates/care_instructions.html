Care Instructions

Keep your handmade bikini looking beautiful

Your bikini is handcrafted with premium materials. With proper care, it will stay vibrant and beautiful for many seasons. Follow these simple guidelines:

Washing

  • Hand wash only in cold water
  • Use a mild detergent
  • Gently squeeze - do not wring or twist
  • Never machine wash

Drying

  • Lay flat to dry in shade
  • Avoid direct sunlight when drying
  • Never tumble dry
  • Do not hang - may stretch

After Swimming

  • Rinse immediately after use
  • Remove chlorine and salt water
  • Pat dry with a towel
  • Wash as soon as possible

General Care

  • Avoid rough surfaces (wood, concrete)
  • Remove before hot tub use
  • Store in a cool, dry place
  • Keep away from oils and lotions

What to Avoid

No bleach or harsh chemicals

No ironing

No dry cleaning

No fabric softener

Pro Tips

  • Apply sunscreen and let it dry completely before putting on your bikini
  • Bring a spare bag to store wet bikinis when traveling
  • Rotate between multiple bikinis to extend their life
  • For crocheted pieces, be extra gentle to maintain the pattern
