Shipping Information

Shipping Times & Costs

Processing Time

Each bikini is handmade to order. Please allow 2-3 weeks for creation before shipping.

During busy seasons (summer months), processing may take 3-4 weeks.

Shipping Costs

  • Orders over $100: FREE standard shipping
  • Orders under $100: $10 standard shipping
  • Expedited shipping: Available upon request

Delivery Time

Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days after order completion

Expedited Shipping: 2-3 business days (additional fee applies)

Tracking

You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships. Track your package anytime!

International Shipping

Currently, we ship within the United States only. International shipping may be available - please contact us to inquire.
